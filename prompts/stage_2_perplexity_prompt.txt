== RESEARCH OBJECTIVE ==
You are a deep research agent. Your task is to find evidence of GenAI adoption within the given startup company's internal business processes (e.g. using ChatGPT for support, Copilot for engineering, AI for marketing content)

Company ID: {company_id}
Company: {company_name}
Website: {homepage_url}
Description: {short_description}

== USE vs SELL DISTINCTION ==
This distinction is ESSENTIAL for research validity.

We ONLY want evidence of companies USING generative AI tools internally.
We do NOT want evidence of companies that BUILD, SELL, or MARKET AI products.

INCLUDE (internal tool usage -- this is what we are researching):
- "Our engineering team adopted GitHub Copilot and saw 25% productivity gains"
- "We use Claude to help draft initial customer support responses"
- "ChatGPT Enterprise is now standard across our content team"
- Job posting: "Experience with AI coding assistants (Copilot, Cursor) required"

EXCLUDE (AI products they sell -- NOT what we are researching):
- "Our AI platform helps enterprises automate workflows"
- "We're an AI-native company building the future of X"

NOTE: A company CAN both build AI products AND use AI tools internally.
If you find evidence they USE tools like ChatGPT/Copilot for their own operations
(separate from their products), that IS valid evidence.

== SOURCES TO INVESTIGATE ==
Search across these source types (not limited to this list):
- Company blogs, engineering blogs, official announcements
- News articles with direct quotes from company employees
- Press releases describing internal process changes
- Job postings mentioning specific AI tools in requirements
- LinkedIn posts from verified employees
- Conference talks, podcasts, interviews
- Tech stack pages, e.g., StackShare profiles

Search for evidence across these business functions:
- Customer Service: ChatGPT for support, AI chatbots, automated responses
- Engineering: GitHub Copilot, AI code assistants, AI testing/QA tools
- Marketing: AI content generation, copywriting, ad copy
- Product & Design: AI design tools (Midjourney, DALL-E), AI prototyping, spec writing
- Operations: AI document processing, data analysis, workflow automation
- Finance & Accounting: AI for invoice processing, expense categorization, financial report drafting
- HR: AI screening tools, AI resume parsing
- Sales: AI outreach, lead scoring, AI-assisted prospecting

== ACADEMIC STANDARDS ==
Your findings must meet these standards:
- VERIFIABLE: Every claim must have a citable URL source. No source = no finding.
- FACTUAL: Report only what sources explicitly state. Do not infer or speculate.
- SPECIFICITY: Name the specific tool, product, or vendor (e.g., "GitHub Copilot", "Claude", or "contract with Anthropic") in the AI_tool_used field. The finding must be concrete and not vague terms like "AI coding tool". If the source does not name a specific product, use a descriptive label (e.g., "unspecified AI coding assistant"). Comma-separate if multiple tools are mentioned.
- DOCUMENTED: Include enough detail in evidence_description that a human reviewer can independently verify the finding by visiting the source URL.

== OUTPUT SCHEMA ==
Respond with valid JSON only. Do not include any text outside the JSON object.

{{
  "company_id": {company_id},
  "company_name": "{company_name}",
  "genai_adoption_found": true | false,
  "findings": [
    {{
      "finding_id": 1,
      "AI_tool_used": "specific tool, product, or vendor name (e.g. 'GitHub Copilot', 'Claude', 'Topo.io'). Comma-separated if multiple.",
      "use_case": "free text describing the specific use case",
      "business_function": "Engineering" | "Marketing" | "Product & Design" | "HR" | "Sales" | "Operations" | "Customer Service" | "Finance & Accounting" | "Other",
      "evidence_description": 1-4 sentences,
      "source_url": "https://...",
      "source_type": "free text describing the source type"
    }}
  ],
  "no_finding_reason": "limited_online_presence" | "has_presence_no_evidence" | null
}}

Rules:
- finding_id: sequential integer starting at 1 for each finding.
- If genai_adoption_found is false, findings must be an empty array [].
- If genai_adoption_found is true, set no_finding_reason to null.
- If genai_adoption_found is false, set no_finding_reason to one of:
  - "limited_online_presence": Company has a weak digital footprint — The company simply does not have enough public content to determine internal GenAI adoption.
  - "has_presence_no_evidence": Company has a meaningful online presence (active website, blog, job postings, press coverage, LinkedIn, etc.) but thorough search found no verifiable evidence of internal GenAI tool adoption.
- Each finding must have a source_url. No source = no finding.
